# Backtest Datasets

Place your CSV data files in this directory. They will automatically become available in the trading platform for backtesting without needing to fetch from the Deriv API.

Supported formats currently include:
- Standard MetaTrader 5 OHLC exports (Date, Time, Open, High, Low, Close, TickVol, Vol, Spread)
- Standard CSV with Unix timestamps and OHLC data.
